Why Study Abroad?

The opportunity to study abroad is one of the most unique and unforgettable aspects of attending college. At Syracuse University, there are dozens of once-in-a-lifetime programs for students to participate in that span the globe. We have programs in a wide range of countries in Europe, Asia, Africa and South America. Our study abroad programs range in length from a few weeks to an entire semester, giving students the opportunity to immerse themselves in a new culture at whatever depth they desire. Three of our most popular study abroad destinations are London, Madrid and Florence. Keep reading to learn more about what each of these programs has to offer!

London

Syracuse University's London program offers a variety of courses in communications, art, humanities and dozens of other areas of study. During their time in the historic and stunning city of London, students will be able to explore the city's iconic landmarks and museums while taking courses at Syracuse University's London Center.

London Program

Madrid

In Madrid, Syracuse University students can improve their Spanish proficiency while immersing themselves the local culture. Madrid hosts a celebrated art scene and a rich history visible in its countless musesums and elegant architecture. In addition to Spanish courses, students can take a variety of courses in history and humanitarian areas such as psychology and sociology.

Madrid Program

Florence

Florence is the perfect destination for Syracuse University students looking to surround themselves with centuries of Italian art, literature and history. Through the Florence program, students will receive the opportunity to explore both the city of Florence and Italy as a whole. Students have the option to learn at Syracuse University's Florence Center, or enroll in courses taught in either English or Italian at a local university.


Florence Program

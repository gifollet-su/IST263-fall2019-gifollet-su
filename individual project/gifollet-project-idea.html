Gillian Follett Individual Project Idea

My website will feature information about a few of the study abroad programs at Syracuse University. I will get information about the programs from the SU website and use stock photos for images.

I will likely focus on three study abroad programs that I’m personally trying to decide between— Madrid, Florence, and London— and give each program its own page.

To avoid any intellectual property issues, I will reword and restructure the text from the SU website and include it on my own site. I will highlight educational information about each site, such as the courses offered, and also describe the attractions located in each city.

Web pages:

Madrid
Florence
London

Page content:

Local attractions
Courses offered
Housing options
Costs

Content sources:

Stock photos
Information from Syracuse University website (text)

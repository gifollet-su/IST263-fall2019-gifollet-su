Welcome to London!

Syracuse University's London Center offers something for every student, whether they are interested in pursuing a career in drama, art and design, communications, or dozens of other areas of study. Located in Bloomsbury, London's academic district, the London Center offers special programs in Architecture, Design, Drama and Computer Science. Between classes, students are encouraged to explore the sprawling, historic city and lose themselves in the broad range of opportunities at their fingertips. Students can also gain further professional experience by taking part in the Syracuse London Internship Program, which offers students the chance to work for a local, national or international organization located in the city.

Attractions

London is home to a rich cultural history and a large number of famous landmarks. During their time in London, students are encouraged to venture beyond the London Center and explore the distinct neighborhoods that make up the city, including Greenwich, Southwark and Notting Hill. Each neighborhood offers a different experience but promises an equally enriching cultural experience.

Housing & Pricing

While living in London, students will reside in flats, rather than in college dorms. These flats are located in a range of different buildings across London, and as such, each is unique from the others. Though flat-living is different from living in a dorm, each building comes with a guide to answer any questions about the electrical devices, showers, and kitchen appliances that the flats are stocked with.
